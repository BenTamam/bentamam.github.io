## About Me

Offensive security researcher at Check Point. I came up through hands-on penetration testing. Today my focus is adversary simulation and red team operations - replicating real threat actor behaviour end-to-end across Windows, Active Directory, and cloud - alongside vulnerability research and AI red teaming.
